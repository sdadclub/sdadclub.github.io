Who are we ?

The SDAD club is a club belonging to the SDAD Master program: Data science and decision support within the Faculty of Sciences and Techniques of Marrakech (FSTG) which seeks to develop professional capacities, scientists, and even personal students in this field which has taken on a large scale, due to the emergence and ever-increasing importance of Big Data and data science.
