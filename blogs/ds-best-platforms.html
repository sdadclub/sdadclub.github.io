Best platforms to learn Data Science

Thu Feb 17 2022 Category : Information Technology

If you want to start learning data science and don't know where to start, you are in the right place. Throughout this article, I will give you the best platforms that can help you start your career.

The field of data science is one of the most growing fields of work these days. Day by day, global companies are increasingly in need of data analysts, data engineers, data scientists, and other people who can handle data. And in order to be one of them, there’s a lot of skills that should be useful if you learn them, like: Statistics, Programming, Databases, Distributed Computing, Machine learning, Visualization, Experimental design, Deep Learning, NPL, Linear algebra, Calculus … etc.

Getting Started is usually the hardest step in your path to learning, so in this article I decided to give you some platforms that should help you make that step and even more trace you the path that you should take so that you won’t be lost.

Let’s get started.

CodeAcademy

Codecademy is an interactive website that provides a platform for learning programming languages such as Python, JavaScript and Ruby as well as coding languages such as HTML and CSS.

There are many free courses to learn from Codecademy. It is worth sign-up for a pro-plan. I always see spending money helps in increasing accountability. Also, the pro-plans get you access to its powerful community.

Codecademy is not just for data science. It is a platform that supports developers across many careers. There are hundreds of courses and it has more than 45 million users.

DataCamp

DataCamp offers interactive R, Python, Sheets, SQL and shell courses. All on topics in data science, statistics and machine learning. Learn from a team of expert teachers in the comfort of your browser with video lessons and fun coding challenges and projects.

Khan Academy

Khan Academy is a non-profit organization that aims to spread academic knowledge to all using the most advanced educational methods. The Academy provides free theoretical resources prepared on an international educational level, and thus is considered the forerunner in this field.

It is the best platform to learn the math concept for data science. It is fine to not understand mathematical concepts when you start learning data science. But, to become a successful data scientist you need to learn mathematical concepts. Math is again another top reason which scares people away from data science. Trust me all you need to know is the high school level of math.

Coursera

Coursera is a for-profit educational technology company that provides massive open courses created by instructors from Stanford University. Coursera works with international universities to offer their courses through the Coursera Educational Systems Management interface. It has more than 75 million Registered users.

There are so many data science-related courses. Just reading about them on the platform will take hours. All of those courses are good. The important factor to consider is which of those courses are good for you.

HackerRank

HackerRank is a technology hiring platform that is the standard for assessing developer skills for over 2,800+ companies around the world. By enabling tech recruiters and hiring managers to objectively evaluate talent at every stage of the recruiting process, HackerRank helps companies hire skilled developers and innovate faster!

It is an amazing platform for software engineers. There are some good challenges to test your skills in,

• Python

• R

• SQL

• Problem Solving

Thanks for reading, I hope this helps you well.
